The mission of Bay Area Volunteers (BAV) is to empower youths to give back to the community. BAV strives to connect youths around the Bay Area to year-round volunteer programs. From volunteering at food banks to helping at animal shelters, volunteering can expose you to very new experiences and meeting new people. We believe everyone can create real impact in their communities. BAV avidly advocates for change in Bay Area Homelessness, connecting them to services that they need to move forward and restart their lives.
